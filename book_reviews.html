Book Reviews

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear

A practical and insightful book on building good habits and breaking bad ones. Clear uses psychology and real-life examples to illustrate his points.

Rating: ★★★★☆

"Deep Work" by Cal Newport

This book explores the value of deep, focused work in an age of distractions. It offers strategies to cultivate focus and achieve meaningful results.

Rating: ★★★★★

"The Lean Startup" by Eric Ries

Essential reading for entrepreneurs and innovators. Ries presents a methodology for building products more efficiently through continuous innovation.

Rating: ★★★★☆
